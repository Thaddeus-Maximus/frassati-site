### Who?

We are a group of young Catholic men in McLean County inspired by [the life of St. Pier Giorgio Frassati.](/about-frassati)

### Why?

Truth, virtue, and justice are the fundamental pillars by which we form men within our fellowship, so that likewise, they may form society.

### What?

We meet weekly to discuss [texts](/reading-list) and how we may implement these ideas into the world.
