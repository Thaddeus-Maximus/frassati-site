Catholic Action Summit

April 25, 8AM-3:30PM @ Holy Trinity

711 N Main Street, Bloomington, IL 61701

We will meet in the church to pray the rosary before moving to the basement. Conference will conclude with usual vigil mass upstairs at 4PM.

Speakers:

  • Dr. Kent Lasnoski, President of San Damiano College of the Trades
  • Dr. Benjamin Keil, Assistant Professor at ISU
  • IWP Capital
  • You, Perhaps? We will have a lightning round of 5 minute talks on projects happening now in our community; sign up to speak today!

Topics:

  • What is Catholic Social Teaching?
  • Where should my money go? What should I invest in?
  • What structures can we use that align more with a Catholic vision of the common good rather than just a secular vision of maximum shareholder profit?
  • What opportunities exist in my community for me to invest in?

Food:

No conference is complete without food. We’ll have soup and sandwiches, served family-style.

RSVP by April 15th, please!
